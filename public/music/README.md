# Background music tracks

Drop royalty-free mp3s here matching the ids in `src/lib/musicLibrary.ts`:

- `upbeat-1.mp3`
- `chill-1.mp3`
- `dramatic-1.mp3`
- `corporate-1.mp3`
- `cinematic-1.mp3`

Good sources for genuinely royalty-free tracks: YouTube Audio Library,
Pixabay Music, Free Music Archive (check each track's specific license).
After adding a file, fill in its `credit` field in `musicLibrary.ts` with
the attribution the license requires.
